About us

We make your dream event realised!

Truddy's Art & Kitchen house is a full service events management and outside catering company located in the heart of Kisumu, Kenya. Our company specializes in creating unique and memorable experiences for our clients through creative events planning, delicious catering and beautiful art elements. With a team of experienced proffesssionals and a passion for deriving exceptional services, Truddy's Art and Kitchen house is ready to take the events industry by a storm.
